Pillsbury's Crescent Roll Taco Bake
This Taco Ring is a great way to turn tacos into an easy to eat game day food!

Ingredients

  • 2 crescent roll tubes
  • 1 LB ground beef or turkey
  • 1 packet of taco seasoning
  • 1 1/2 cups grated chedder cheese
  • Shredded lettuce
  • 1 or 2 diced tomatos depending on size
  • 1/2 small can sliced olives if desired
  • Sour cream optional
  • sliced avacado optional

Instructions

  1. Lay out the 2 tubes of crescent pastry, thick side facing in.
  2. Use some of the left over crescent rolls to make the center a bit thicker.
  3. Brown beef and add taco seasoning.
  4. Lay beef in a circle inside the laid out crescent rolls.
  5. Add cheese to the top
  6. Pull over crescent rolls and tuck in under meat and cheese.
  7. Add cheese, lettuce, tomato, black olives, sour cream or whatever you would like in your tacos, in the middle.
  8. Follow cook time on the pack of crescent rolls and once it's done, enoy your tacos!
